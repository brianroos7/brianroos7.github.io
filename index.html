What We Offer

End-to-End IFC Consultancy

From initial strategic advice through to full account management and customer reporting — we work alongside airlines, business aviation and head of state operators as a trusted, independent IFC partner.

IFC Advisory

Independent, vendor-neutral guidance from day one. We assess your connectivity requirements, evaluate provider options across the full LEO and GEO solution landscape, and build a clear strategy tailored to your operation — with no commercial bias.

  • LEO & GEO connectivity solution evaluation — including the latest low-latency LEO and high-capacity GEO technologies from today's leading providers
  • Operator & passenger demand analysis — understanding what your crew and passengers actually need from connectivity
  • Fleet requirements assessment — matching the right solution to your aircraft type, route network, and operational profile
  • Owner, charter & VIP advisory — tailored guidance for business aviation and head of state operators with bespoke connectivity needs

Account Management

We manage your IFC provider relationship on your behalf — acting as your dedicated point of contact, holding providers accountable, and ensuring your interests are represented at every stage.

Reporting & Liaison

Clear, accurate, and timely reporting back to your team. We bridge the gap between your operation and your IFC service provider — translating technical data into actionable insight you can act on.
