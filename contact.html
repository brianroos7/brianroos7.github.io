What to Expect

How We Work Together

What information should I include in my first message?
A brief overview of your organisation, your current IFC provider or setup, the challenge you're facing, and your expected timeline is enough to get started. We'll follow up with any additional questions during our initial call.
Do you work with operators outside of your home country?
Yes — we work with airlines, business aviation and head of state operators internationally. Many engagements are conducted remotely, with on-site visits arranged where the scope requires it.
How long does a typical engagement take?
It depends on scope. An initial advisory engagement can take a few weeks; ongoing account management is typically structured as a retainer. We'll give you a clear proposal after our initial consultation.
Do you offer one-off advisory calls?
Yes. If you need a focused expert opinion without committing to a longer engagement, we're happy to arrange an hourly advisory session.
